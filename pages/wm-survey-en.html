Government of Canada web management questionnaire

The Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat (TBS) and Service Canada (SC) are seeking input from departments to assess current and future needs in web management with the goal of delivering, monitoring and continuously improving services to Canadians.

This questionnaire will provide:

  • a point-in-time snapshot of Government of Canada web publishing technology and development capacity
  • anticipated departmental web publishing and search needs

Deadline

Please complete this survey on or before Friday, November 9, 2018.
